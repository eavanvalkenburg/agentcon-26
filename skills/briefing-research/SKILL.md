# Briefing research

Use this skill when you need to gather and structure research notes for a short briefing.

## Process

1. Restate the topic and the intended audience.
2. Identify the core facts the audience needs to understand.
3. Capture practical implications, not just definitions.
4. Call out risks, unknowns, and trade-offs.
5. End with demo angles that can be shown on screen.

## Output shape

Return:

- Key facts
- Audience needs
- Risks and mitigations
- Practical implications
- Demo angles

Use `read_skill_resource` for `references/research-checklist.md` when you need a compact checklist.
