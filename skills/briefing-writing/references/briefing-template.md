# Briefing template

## Title

Short, specific, and audience-facing.

## Three takeaways

1. What changed?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What should the audience do next?

## Demo narrative

- Start with the problem.
- Show the agent/workflow/harness behavior.
- End with the outcome and concept it proves.

## Audience question

Ask one question that starts discussion instead of checking comprehension.
