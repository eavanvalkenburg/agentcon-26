# Briefing writing

Use this skill when research needs to become a concise, presenter-ready briefing.

## Process

1. Choose a clear title.
2. Write three takeaways that can fit on a slide.
3. Add a short demo narrative with a beginning, middle, and end.
4. Include one audience engagement question.
5. Keep language direct and easy to read aloud.

## Output shape

Return:

- Title
- Three takeaways
- Demo narrative
- Audience question

Use `read_skill_resource` for `references/briefing-template.md` when drafting the final structure.
